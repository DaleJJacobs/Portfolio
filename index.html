About Me

Born in 1980 to a middle-class, white suburban family in Delware County, PA, I started a fairly normal suburban childhood. That was of course until my parents got divorced when I was around the age of 10, which I guess is pretty normal actually. I won some writing awards and became that creepy kid always writing a journal or bad poetry. I didn't know what I wanted to do out of High School, so while in Community College I found Univeristy of the Arts! Where I studied Screenwriting. I discovered a love for post-production in TV and that led me to jobs with NFL Films and NBCUniversal. I've recently decided to change careers so here I am at UPenn studying Web Development!

I'm very huggable.
